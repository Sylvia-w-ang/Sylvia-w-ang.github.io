My Mission

I believe that culture is meant to be shared — not just preserved.

Fortune telling is a vibrant part of Taiwanese tradition, and my mission is to reintroduce it as a joyful, meaningful experience for people everywhere.

This isn’t just about telling fortunes.

It’s about creating moments of connection — laughter between friends, reflection with family, and curiosity that crosses borders.

Whether it's reading fortunes with friends or bonding with family over playful predictions, my goal is to spark meaningful conversations, laughter, and a little magic.
